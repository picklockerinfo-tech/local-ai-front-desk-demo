About Us

We’re a local mobile detailing business serving Vallejo, Fairfield and Concord. Whether you need a quick wash, deep clean or full interior detail, our pros bring the shine to your doorstep. No waiting rooms, no hassle — just professional service with a smile.

Our Services

  • Basic Wash – exterior wash & quick interior wipe.
  • Premium Detail – wash, wax, vacuum & windows.
  • Deluxe Interior – deep clean of seats, carpets & dashboard.
  • Fleet Service – custom packages for business vehicles.
